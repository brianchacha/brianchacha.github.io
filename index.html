    1. Lost and Found Authentication App

    The Lost and Found Verification App is a mobile application designed to help students and staff of educational institutions to locate lost items easily. The app requires students to log in using their authentication details, after which they can fill in a form with details of their lost items. Staff members will then check the system for lost items that match the description given by the student, and if there is a match, the student will be notified to come to the staff's office to collect their item.

    The app will require the student to verify his account and enter his log in details that were registered and saved in the firebase authentication database, make a request of a lost item by filling a form with the description of the item. When his item has been validated by the staff to be available in the lost and found collections, the student is notified using an SMS sent with the help of the app. Finally the after the whole process is completed, students are required to make a payment through MPESA using the app

    I also implemented Daraja Lipa na Mpesa STK Push in my app to enable students to make payment of service using directly without having to use an MPESA app or any other third party app
